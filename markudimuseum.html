Makurdi Museum

City: Makurdi

Country/Territory: Nigeria

Address: Makurdi Museum, Makurdi, Nigeria

About Makurdi Museum The National Museum in Makurdi the Benue State Capital was established 1989, although the building it occupies was built in 1929 by the British. Get to know more about the ancient idols/gods worshipped by the Benue people before the acceptance of Christianity and Islam. Before leaving the Museum cease the opportunity to feed the crocodiles!

Price: ₦90,000
