Enumabia Warm Spring

City: Makurdi


Country/Territory: Nigeria


Address: Enumabia Warm Spring

About: The Enemabia warm spring is more or less the only warm spring in Benue State. Tourists and people alike can swim in its waters and enjoy the warming of natural spring. It is open night and day to visitors and it is a place anyone must check out once they visit the state.
